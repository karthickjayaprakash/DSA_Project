ABOUY

SEEDLING  was established with the aim of providing quality  education on par with international standards.
 It persistently seeks and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of education on a consistent basis.
The campus has a cosmopolitan atmosphere with students from all corners of the globe. 
Experienced and learned teachers are strongly encouraged to nurture the students. 
The global standards set at SEEOLING in the field of teaching and research spur us on in our relentless pursuit of excellence.
In fact, it has become a way of life for us.
 The highly motivated youngsters on the campus are a constant source of pride.
 Our Memoranda of Understanding with various international schools are our major strength. 
They provide for an exchange of students and faculty and encourage joint research projects for the mutual benefit of these schools. 
Many of our students, who pursue their research projects in foreign schools, bring high quality to their work and esteem to India and have done us proud. 
With steady steps, we continue our march forward. We look forward to meeting you here at SEEDLING.


History of SEEDLING

It was established in 1956, and was founded in 1984 as a self-financing institution called the SEEDLING school.
 The Union Ministry of Human Resources Development conferred schools status  in 2001.
