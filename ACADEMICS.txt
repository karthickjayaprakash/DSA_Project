SEEDLING  offers WORLD CLASS EDUCATION . The structure of the curriculum in all these programmes follows the pattern of ‘CENTRAL BOARD OF SECONDARY EDUCATION (CBCE)’ 
 (a) core papers
 (b) projects
 (c) discipline specific electives
 (d) generic electives
 (e) ability enhancement
 (f) skill enhancement and 
 (g) co and extracurricular activities. 
Credit transfer option is also given after successful completion of SEEDLING approved list of courses that are available in the SWAYAM platform.

The curriculum of all the programmes focusses more on ‘learning by doing’ and therefore has embedded regular laboratory classes and the projects, as per the requirement.
